2GLL (under construction)

84 algorithms are needed to complete the last layer in 1 look. They are divided into 8 subsets based on corner orientation.
To minimize redundancy, algorithms that are mirrors of previously shown ones are always placed at the end each subset.

Since many algorithms are made of combinations of sunes, the following abbreviations will be used to aid memorization:

  • S - Sune
  • S' - Antisune
  • BS - Backsune
  • BS' - Backantisune
